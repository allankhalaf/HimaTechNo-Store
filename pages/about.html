Our Story

From a small shop to a leading tech retailer

Founded in 2021 in the heart of Damascus, HimaTech-Store started with a simple mission: to make technology accessible to everyone in Syria. What began as a small shop has grown into one of the most trusted tech e-commerce platforms in the region.

We carefully curate our product selection to ensure we only offer genuine, high-quality tech products from reputable brands. From smartphones and laptops to smart home devices and accessories, we have everything tech enthusiasts need.

Our team of tech experts is always ready to help you find the perfect product for your needs and budget.
