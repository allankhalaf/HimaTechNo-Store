How Can We Help You?

Find answers to the most common questions about our products, orders, shipping, and more.

Placing an order is simple! Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay securely using various payment methods. Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive an email with your order details and tracking information.

If you need any assistance during the process, feel free to contact our support team.

Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section.

Please allow 24-48 hours for tracking information to update after receiving the shipping confirmation.

You can cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it, provided it hasn't been processed for shipping yet. To cancel, go to "My Orders" in your account and click the cancel button next to your order.

If the order has already been shipped, you will need to wait for delivery and then initiate a return.

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days (Free for orders over $50)
Express Shipping: 2-3 business days ($15)
Next Day Delivery: 1 business day ($25)

Shipping times may vary based on your location in Syria and product availability.

Currently, we primarily ship within Syria. However, we are working on expanding our shipping services to neighboring countries soon. Stay tuned for updates!

For special international shipping requests, please contact our customer support team.

Shipping costs depend on your location, the weight of your items, and the shipping method you choose. Standard shipping is free for all orders over $50 within Syria.

You can see the exact shipping cost at checkout before completing your purchase.

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in original condition with all packaging and accessories. Some items like software, personalized products, and consumables may not be eligible for return.

To initiate a return, go to "My Orders" and select the return option, or contact our support team.

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method and bank:

Credit/Debit Card: 5-10 business days
PayPal: 3-5 business days
Bank Transfer: 7-14 business days

We accept a variety of payment methods for your convenience:

Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express
Digital Wallets: PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
Cash on Delivery: Available for orders under $500 within Syria
Bank Transfer: Available for large orders
